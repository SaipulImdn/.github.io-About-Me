ABOUT

My name is Syaiful Imanudin. I was born in Cirebon on 23 September 2002, I attended school in Cirebon since elementary school, and now I am a student at one of the universities in my city, namely STMIK Cirebon. I majored in informatics engineering, why did I major in informatics engineering because I want to learn about technology that is developing for the present and the future.

I started elementary school at SDN 1 Kalijaga Permai then when I was in grade 1 semester 1 I moved schools to SDN 2 Tukmudal, then I went to school there until I finished elementary school, then I entered the first middle school at SMP 1 Sumber I continued school there until I finished , then I continued high school at MAN 2 Cirebon City, when there I learned a lot of new things about religion and various other things, I also had time to participate in various organizations there such as OSIS and MPK, I had served as chairman of the student council there. Then after that we studied online due to the Covid-19 pandemic and it happened until we graduated from school. Then I continued my studies at one of the campuses in the city of Cirebon, namely STIMK IKMI Cirebon. I'm starting a new journey where there are so many things I don't know, and now I'm trying to graduate quickly.
